About the Project

Step into a world where history meets rhythm in Rhythm Warriors: Time Warp, an exhilarating VR game compatible with Meta Quest 2. This innovative gaming experience transports players to a village cursed with a mystical time rift, where they become the heroes tasked with closing the rift and restoring the timeline.

At its core, Rhythm Warriors: Time Warp is not just a game; it's a captivating journey through historical eras, seamlessly blending education with entertainment. Players embark on a rhythmic adventure, defeating enemies in sync with a dynamic soundtrack, collecting artifact pieces, and actively participating in the restoration of the village. The game's immersive design allows users to explore diverse historical settings, interacting meaningfully with NPCs for a truly engaging experience.

What sets Rhythm Warriors: Time Warp apart is its revolutionary approach to learning. It caters to students, history enthusiasts, and a wide audience seeking an interactive education, turning history into an enjoyable and memorable experience. The rhythm-based combat, combined with the rich narrative, ensures that players not only absorb historical knowledge but also have a blast doing so. Embark on this extraordinary journey, where every tap of the drum propels you deeper into the rhythm of time.

This project was developed as part of the CS 426 Senior Projects in Computer Science course at the University of Nevada, Reno during Fall 2023/Spring 2024.
